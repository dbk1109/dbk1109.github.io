MATAVA

Website / Logo / Business card / Document format design

MATAVA is a eco-friendly resort located in Fiji.
They wanted a playful site that wasn't modern. By boldly choosing the main font, which determines the overall atmosphere of the site, I have satisfied the fun and free atmosphere that they want.

Logo Renewal Proposal

They required a change in logo configuration to use of both ways, horizontal and vertical, away from the inflexible and old atmosphere.
So I remove the tilted parts from the original logo, not only giving them a static element, but also provided variations on the basic skeletal system for use on holidays and on important days.

Business Card Design

They hoped to have an eco-friendly business card.
I researched as they preferred the craft than white paper. So I recommended a company that uses 100% recycled paper and veggie ink to them, and they were satisfied. I recommended later a corrugated paper that could take a trend through letterpress printing technique from another company, they were satisfied with it as well.
