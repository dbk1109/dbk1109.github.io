E-planet

Website / Outdoor sign / Truck wrapping design

E-planet is a scrap metal recycling company based in Sydney.
They wanted to get out of the image of 'scrap is dirty'. They wanted to use colors rather than plain white to give them a clean look. So I chose their colors blue and green such as their logo colors. It will strengthen their identity.

Truck Wrapping Design

They offered a free pick-up service. In the branding process, they were contemplating ways to inform more potential customers about their company, and I let them know that there was a way to advertise by truck. I suggested them a design and it generated their positive response.
